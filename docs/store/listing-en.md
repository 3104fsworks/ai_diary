## Long description

```
"I never manage to keep a diary."

This app is for you.

──────

■ Who it's for

- People who want to journal but never stick with it
- Busy people who don't have time at a desk every night
- Anyone who wants to reflect a little more gently on each day

──────

■ What it does

🎙️ Speak for a moment
You don't have to look at the screen.
The mic keeps listening until you tap again — pause,
look up, think. The AI stays patient.

📷 Up to 3 photos
Add the visual moments. The AI quietly describes
each one in the diary.

🎯 Today's goals
Tick the small intentions you set for yourself —
"5,000 steps", "say thank you", "sleep before midnight".

📊 Data gathered in the background
Steps (via Health Connect / Apple Health), calendar events,
completed tasks, place timeline — all quietly collected.

✨ Press Done. That's it.
The AI weaves your day into a first-person diary entry.

──────

■ Design notes

- Stoic, editorial minimalism — never playful
- Light and dark themes, 8 accent colours
- Text size in 4 steps (readable at any age)

──────

■ Your own words, preserved

A "Today's whisper" section keeps the raw voice transcript
verbatim — exactly what you said, never rewritten.
However polished the AI version, your real voice stays.

──────

■ Choose your AI's voice (Premium)

- Standard (polite, intellectual)
- Mirror your voice (matches your tone and rhythm)
- Warm best friend (gentle and affirming)

On a heavy day, the AI dials down its brightness
and simply sits beside you.

──────

■ Privacy

All diary data stays on your device
(or in your own iCloud / Google Drive folder).
We never see your diaries.

Export to Obsidian or Notion any time.

──────

■ Pricing

- Free plan: typed entries, one AI generation per day
- Voice plan: ¥300/mo (¥2,900/yr)
- Voice + photo plan: ¥500/mo (¥4,900/yr)
- Full plan: ¥1,000/mo (¥9,800/yr)

First 10 entries on Full plan are free.

If you bring your own Gemini API key, AI is unlimited
regardless of plan.
```
